# ⏱️ 90-Second Demo Flow: Project CLEARSIGHT

Welcome, Judge (or "Operative"). This agent runs completely serverless via `clawless`. Follow this script to see the core value and the ARG mechanics in under 90 seconds.

### 1. The Core Utility: Digital Recon [15s]
* **Action:** Paste the following text directly into the chat:
  > *"Warning: Your account will be deleted in 10 minutes if you don't upgrade to Premium. 98% of users upgrade to stay safe."*
* **Watch For:** The agent generates a **Debrief Scorecard**. Notice the precise Evidence Extraction, the Confidence Score, and the **Fair Rewrite** showing an ethical alternative.

### 2. Gamified Training: Physical Recon [20s]
* **Action:** Type: *"I need a training mission."* * **Watch For:** The browser requests location access. Approve it. The `locate-node` tool pings OpenStreetMap, finds a real business category near you, and drafts a localized espionage training brief.

### 3. The Digital Sabotage [25s]
* **Action:** Respond to the mission brief with this mock Terms of Service snippet:
  > *"By clicking agree, you consent to sharing your contact list with our trusted partners. To opt out, please mail a written letter to our headquarters."*
* **Watch For:** The agent analyzes the text, awards you "Sabotage Points" (saved locally), and breaks down the 'Roach Motel' Dark Pattern.

### 4. Failsafe Demonstration [15s]
* **Action:** Deny location access. Type *"Give me another mission."*
* **Watch For:** The app won't crash. Our dynamic timezone fallback detects your region and automatically assigns a mission in a major commercial hub in your timezone.
